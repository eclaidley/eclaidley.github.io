Innovative Protein Technologies

Innovative Protein Technologies (IPT) is a biotechnology company dedicated to producing environmentally-friendly crop protection products. Their first product, Frost Armour, protects crops against the devastating effects of frost damage.

As the co-founder and Chief Marketing Officer, I was involved in a number of projects, including lab work, marketing, fundraising, and web development.
